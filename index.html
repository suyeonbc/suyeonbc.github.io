Suyeon Jeong

I am an undergraduate student at Seoul National University, majoring in Mechanical Engineering. I was honored to be awarded the Presidential Science Scholarship.

My research lies at the convergence of Materials Science and Robotics, with a particular focus on soft robotics and adaptive systems. I also worked as an R&D Lead at Solive Ventures, a startup developing soft sensory hardware and mobile application for home-based child development.

I envision a future where adaptable, compliant machines and robots play an essential role in society, revolutionizing healthcare devices, haptic interfaces, and human-machine interaction systems.
